Vertix Servies

By Blake AlfordMay 14th 2021

What is Vertix Services and why have we added it? To simplify that we have big plans for the future and restricting it all to one brand name, "Vertix Studios" restricts us from being able to move forward with these ideas and plans, as of right now the Vertix Services branch is still in its development phase and will continue to develop over the coming months and years.

The idea of the Vertix Services branch was to provide users with top notch services for people to use for free, yes correct free. As a developer hosting is one of the most challenging things when you get ready to release your application to the world, hosting can cost a hefty amount of money, and some free services rate limit you to a specific amount of hours your application can be hosted for. So here at Vertix we created Vertix Hosting (Vertix Services) which is a free hosting tool that developers or anyone can use for any type of project. 

The servers them selves are provided by DanBot Hosting and will keep your bot up 24/7, and even really low ping so your application doesn't take ages to respond or load. (Subject to downtime, Node failures and Server Updates) All servers are equipped with a Memory (RAM) Total of 128mb and a 1000mb Disk Storage, these specs can be increased upon request but will cost you a little bit of money monthly as long as your application is still running.

If your not down for paying that fee you can upgrade to Vertix Pro which is a subscription based service that comes with more then just upgraded Memory (RAM) and Disk Storage, it comes with early access to projects, exclusive Vertix Services, Priority Support and Monthly Analytics.
